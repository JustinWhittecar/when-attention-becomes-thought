## Narrative job

Introduce the transformer architecture and the self-attention mechanism. Show why replacing recurrence with full parallelism changed the relationship between compute and capability, setting the stage for the scaling era.

## Reading list

1. Hochreiter & Schmidhuber (1997), "Long Short-Term Memory." The predecessor.
2. Bahdanau et al. (2014), "Neural Machine Translation by Jointly Learning to Align and Translate." The attention mechanism as a patch on RNNs.
3. Vaswani et al. (2017), "Attention Is All You Need." The central paper.
4. Zhao et al. (2023), "A Survey of Large Language Models." Context on the architecture's downstream impact.
